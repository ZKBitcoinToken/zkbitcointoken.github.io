How Do I Stake?

Participate in Staking

A MetaMask Account with Ethereum on zk Sync Era is required to stake.

Getting zkBitcoin

To get the zkBitcoin / ETH LP Token you need ETH and zkBitcoin in equal parts, 1:1 ratio in price.  
A)  You can bridge ETH over from Ethereum @ zk Sync Era Bridge Here
B)  You get zkBitcoin Tokens via bidding in Auctions on the DAPP or by swapping with SyncSwap @ Click here for SyncSwap buying zkBitcoin, you must add zkBitcon manually to SyncSwap
C)  Once you have both zkBitcoin and ETH tokens from (A) and (B) you can move on to supply Liquidity via SyncSwap

Getting zkBitcoin/ETH SyncSwap Liquidiy Pool Tokens

Click here for Adding to zkBitcoin & ETH liquidity to Syncswap

To stake we need to get the zkBTC/ETH Liquidity Token from SyncSwap. (Use Links above)
Click Deposit
Choose the amount you wish to LP.
Supply the tokens and recieve the LP tokens

After recieving the LP Token you can move on to Depositing the LP Tokens into the Staking Contract

Depositing the zkBitcoin/ETH LP Tokens into the Staking Contract

zkBitcoin Staking example screenshot
zkBitcoin Staking example 2 screenshot
To stake we use the Staking Contract zkBitcoin Staking ETH dAPP
To stake you simply supply zkBitcoin/ETH LP Tokens into the DAPP and receive your fair share of rewards.
Click here to go to zkBTC/ETH LP Staking dAPP
On the DAPP Page click Staking Tab at the top.

Approve the Staking contract to Deposit your LP Tokens in Metamask.
Then approve the Deposit Transaction in Metamask.

Reverse these steps for withdrawing from contracts.

How do rewards get paid to stakers?

40% of the total zkBitcoin goes to stakers!
No dev fee or any sort of fee is taken. All Cryptocurrencies are distributed 100% to the zkBitcoin community.

How Do I Auction?

Auctions only accept Ethereum on zk Sync Era Network.

How to enter and auction

Go to the Auctions dAPP Page. Auctions DAPP Link 
Connect Metamask. Metamask example screenshot
Go to the Auctions dAPP
Select how much Ethereum you want to bid on the auctions.
Bid for the Auction, then allow approve and submit transactions.

or

Just send Ethereum to the Auction contract and you automatically bid on the current auction!!!
ABAS Bid example screenshot

Advanced Auctions - Multiple Future Auctions bidding in one transaction.

Select the advanced tab in the DAPP
Input the starting day to bid on (must be greater than the current day and era)
Input the Total number of auctions. This is how many auctions you want to bid on
Input the Era to bid on (current era = 1)
Bid Ethereum For the Auction button will have an approve and a submit two transactions
zkBitcoin Advanced Auctions example screenshot
No dev fee or any sort of fee is taken. All Cryptocurrencies are distributed 100% to the zkBitcoin community.

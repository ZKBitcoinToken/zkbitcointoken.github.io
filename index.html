Proof of Work Contract

Proof of Work will distribute 100% of the total zkBitcoin Token Supply 

A way to securely mint zkBitcoin tokens using our safe POW system and contracts.
Machines connected to the contract solve complicated hashes with an answer unique to their ethereum address for the reward.
Miners recieve 50 zkBitcoin per mint able to bundle the mints up to 2048 times in one transaction!

MultiMinting custom configurations change MaxZKBTCperMint and MinZKBTCperMint to decide how frequently to mint your bundle of mints in 1 transaction.

To do so just change "MinZKBTCperMint": 500, in the _zkBitcoinMiner.conf text file.

Change this setting in your _zkBitcoinMiner.conf file by changing the "MinZKBTCperMint" setting.
To Solo mine you must fund your account with Ethereum and supply the privateKey to _zkBitcoinMiner.conf in order to mint with a GPU, we suggest a fresh account with just mining funds
